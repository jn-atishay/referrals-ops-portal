Campaign Controls Planned

The end goal: CX resolves the common reward tickets themselves — change a customer's campaign, nudge their wallet credit, resend the gift-form email — without a TechOps ping. The forms, fields and validation are built here as the target UX. The backend calls and audit log get wired in a later phase.

🎛️
UI provisioned, backend not wired. Submitting any form below shows a WIP notice — nothing is sent. Today these actions still run through #referrals-queries → TechOps (see the playbook). Every action here will one day write to an audit log with the operator, timestamp and reason.

Move a referral onto another campaign's gift (e.g. an approved cross-campaign swap). The change is made on the referee's account — see playbook R1.1 and R1.4.

Required before approval — the ledger change is on the referee.
Will be read live once wired.
List will be populated from the campaign config.
Backend not wired
Reminder
Changing the campaign ID does not send the gift-form email on its own — it's sent by hand (playbook R6.1). Use the "Send gift-form email" action below after the change.

Action · PlannedAdd / reduce wallet credits

Adjust a customer's Skydo wallet credit — e.g. grant a $30 referral credit that didn't post, or correct a mistake. Referral credit is fee credit, not cash (playbook R4.2).

Promotional credits belong to other teams (playbook R4.3).
Backend not wired

Action · PlannedSend the gift-form email

Trigger the gift-form (address) email to a qualified referrer. Today this is a manual send — this action replaces that. See playbook R6.1.

Backend not wired
Why this matters
The gift-form email being sent by hand is the single most common silent failure — a campaign ID gets changed but no mail goes out, so the customer never fills the form and the gift never ships. Automating this send closes that gap.

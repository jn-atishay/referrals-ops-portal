How Gifts Are TrackedThe tracking-ID scheme across gifts

Every gift threads through the same set of IDs, from qualification to delivery. This is the map for tying a parcel back to a customer.

ID / fieldWhat it isWhere it livesLinks to
exporter_idThe customer (referrer who won the gift).Every sheet · the analytics toolThe whole chain — always lead with it.
campaign_idWhich campaign's gift applies (this month's gift campaign).Campaign config sheetgift & eligibility
Gift / SKUThe physical item and its stock-keeping unit.Inventory sheetvendor, stock, PO
Form submissionThe referrer's address response. Dispatch starts only after this.Gift-form responses sheetdispatch row
Delivery IDThe dispatch/consignment reference for the parcel.Dispatch & tracking sheetdelivery partner tracking
Delivery partnerCourier handling the parcel; where the customer-facing tracking lives.Dispatch & tracking sheetpartner's tracking site
PO #Purchase order the gift was bought under (for invoice/warranty).Finance & PO trackervendor invoice, warranty
The chain, in one line
exporter_id → campaign_id → gift/SKU → form address → delivery ID + partner → PO#. If any link is missing for a gift that should have shipped, that's the escalation (playbook R3.2).
Worked example
A Coffee Machine and a Mokobara gift were both raised as "no tracking" — i.e. no delivery ID row in the dispatch sheet. Naming the exporter_id + gift is what makes these actionable.
